Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 17, 2025

Welcome to Zk-ShieldNet. Your privacy is not just a policy for us; it is the fundamental principle upon which our technology is built. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information, but more importantly, how our service is designed from the ground up to protect your sensitive data.

The Zk-ShieldNet Service: Privacy-by-Design

The core Zk-ShieldNet Collective Defense Network (the "Service") is architected to ensure we, Zk-ShieldNet Inc., never see, process, or store our customers' sensitive operational data. Our on-premise ZK-Encoder converts raw security telemetry into cryptographic zero-knowledge proofs. Only these anonymous, non-reversible proofs are transmitted to the network.

To be perfectly clear: **Your raw logs, proprietary code, user data, and other confidential information never leave your security perimeter.** This is not a promise; it is a cryptographic guarantee.

Information We Collect (Website Visitors)

This policy primarily concerns our public-facing website (zkshield.net) and our direct business interactions. When you visit our website or interact with us for sales, support, or career opportunities, we may collect the following information:

  • Contact Information: When you request a demo, contact us, or apply for a job, we collect information you provide, such as your name, work email, company name, and job title.
  • Technical Information: Like most websites, we collect standard technical information such as your IP address, browser type, and operating system for analytics and security purposes.
  • Usage Information: We may collect information about how you interact with our website, such as pages visited and time spent, to help us improve our content and user experience.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected from our website visitors is used for the following legitimate business purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries and demo requests.
  • To provide you with customer support.
  • To process job applications.
  • To improve our website and marketing efforts.
  • To maintain the security and integrity of our website.

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect the personal information we handle. This includes encryption, access controls, and secure development practices. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
